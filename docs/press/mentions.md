> **Update via:** `bash scripts/add-press-mention.sh` (interactive prompt)
> or edit this file directly. Either way, the table below is canonical.

## How to use

When a press mention publishes:

1. Add a row to the table below (or run the helper script)
2. Save a PDF copy of the article in `docs/press/archive/<YYYY-MM-DD>-<publication-slug>.pdf`
   (web pages disappear; PDFs in version control don't)
3. Update the milestone tracker at the bottom
4. If it's a Tier-1 publication, mention it in the next investor update

## Tier definitions

- **Tier 1** — Mainstream business / national press: Mint, ET (Economic Times), Forbes India, BusinessLine, Bloomberg, Hindustan Times, BBC, NYT, Bloomberg, Wall Street Journal, Times of India, The Hindu
- **Tier 2** — Tech / startup press: YourStory, Inc42, MoneyControl, Tech in Asia, FactorDaily, Entrepreneur India, ETPrime, BW Disrupt
- **Tier 3** — Niche / vertical: The Better India, BehindWoods, Outlook, regional press, women's-focused publications, travel-vertical sites, podcasts (transcribed)

Wikipedia / Wikidata count Tier-1 and Tier-2 toward notability. Tier-3 builds
brand but doesn't gate Wikipedia eligibility.

## Why this matters more than founders realize

Press mentions compound:

1. **Wikipedia editors check this list.** A Wikipedia article filed with 8
   citations is approved 60%+ of the time; with 3 citations it's approved
   <10% (per Wikipedia's own AfC stats).
2. **AI engines weight Wikipedia heavily.** A Wikipedia entry routinely shows
   up in Perplexity / ChatGPT / Claude citations. The path is: press →
   Wikipedia → AI citation graph.
3. **Investor follow-on rounds care.** Tier-1 press in your last 6 months
   reads as legitimacy at Series A diligence.
4. **Brand SERP defense.** When someone Googles "Wander Women," what shows
   up matters. Press mentions push down random Wander Women lookalikes.

## Press strategy reminder

Pitch one story per quarter. Different angle each time:

- Q1: Founder story + product launch
- Q2: Women's safety in Indian travel — data-driven (Beware Board insights)
- Q3: AI verification + DPDP compliance — tech-policy angle
- Q4: Milestone (1000 paid members, 100 contributors, etc.)

Pre-write a 250-word pitch for each angle. Customize per publication. Send
one journalist per pub at a time (not blast emails).
