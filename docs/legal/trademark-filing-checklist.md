# Trademark filing — checklist and ready-to-send brief

> Goal: file the Indian trademark application for "Wander Women" within
> 14 days. Total cost: ~₹14,000 (₹9,000 govt fee × 1 class, ₹4,500 attorney fee).
> File first, perfect later.

## Why this is urgent

1. **Brand on rented land** — until filed, anyone can claim the mark and
   force a rebrand at scale. A rebrand 18 months in costs ₹40L+ in equity,
   SEO, and physical/digital reprints.
2. **Diligence flag** — every Series-A investor's outside counsel will ask
   *"is the mark filed?"* The answer cannot be "no."
3. **Madrid Protocol priority** — international filings inherit your Indian
   priority date. The earlier the Indian filing, the cheaper the global one.

### 2. Decide the classes

Indian trademark classes are based on the Nice Classification. File in
the classes where you actively offer or will offer services within 5 years.

**Recommended classes:**

| Class | Description | Why for Wander Women |
|---|---|---|
| **39** | Travel arrangement, travel information, escorting of travellers | Core product — trip planning, intel, buddy matching |
| **41** | Education, training, entertainment, online publication of electronic books and journals | Trip Intel Cards, Beware Board, contributor content, community |
| **42** | Software as a service, hosting of platforms | The tech platform itself, MCP/API |

**Do NOT file class 9 (downloadable software)** — you don't ship binaries,
and class 42 (SaaS) is the right fit for a hosted platform.

**Do NOT over-file** — every class is ₹9,000 government fee. Three classes
is the right scope at our stage. Add classes 35 (advertising / e-commerce)
and 16 (printed publications) only when revenue genuinely materializes there.

### 3. Decide the form of the mark

- **Word mark only** (recommended for filing #1) — ₹9,000 per class. Protects
  the words "Wander Women" in any font/color. Most flexible. File this first.
- **Logo mark / device mark** — ₹9,000 per class. Protects a specific design.
  File later (round 2) once your logo is final and stable.
- **Combined word + logo** — same fee. Often weakest because it only protects
  the exact combination.

**Recommendation:** File the word mark in three classes immediately. File
the logo as a separate application within 6 months once branding is final.

### 5. Applicant entity

- Application MUST be filed by the entity that will own the mark.
- If Pvt Ltd is incorporated: **file under the Pvt Ltd**.
- If pre-incorporation: **file under the founder's name** and assign to the
  Pvt Ltd post-incorporation (this is standard but adds ~₹3,000 in
  assignment-recording fees later).

**Recommendation:** wait for Pvt Ltd incorporation if it's < 30 days away.
Otherwise file under founder's name and assign later — the priority date
matters more than entity tidiness.

## Filing process (~14 days)

1. **Day 0** — Engage a trademark agent / attorney. Recommended:
   - LegalRaasta, Vakilsearch, or IndiaFilings (cheap; quality varies)
   - K&S Partners, Anand and Anand (premium; for high-stakes filings)
   - Direct filing on <https://ipindia.gov.in/> if comfortable (you'll need
     a digital signature certificate)
2. **Day 1** — Knockout search + classes + description finalized.
3. **Day 2–3** — Form TM-A filed online for each class.
4. **Day 3–5** — Government fee paid; receipt + filing date confirmed.
   This is your priority date — keep the receipt.
5. **Day 5–14** — Trade Marks Registry examines for conflicts and assigns an
   examiner. You may receive an examination report; respond within 30 days.
6. **Month 4–18** — If no objections, mark is published in the TM Journal for
   public opposition. 4 months opposition window.
7. **Month 18–24** — If no opposition, registration certificate issued.

You can use ™ symbol from day of filing. Use ® only after registration certificate.

## Madrid Protocol (international expansion — Phase 2)

When ready to expand internationally:
- Cost: ~CHF 1,000 base + ~CHF 100 per designated country
- Recommended initial designations: US, UK, EU, Australia, Singapore
- Total estimate: ~₹2L for the full filing
- File within 6 months of Indian priority date for priority chaining

**Trigger:** when foreign user signups cross 5% of total, or when contributor
acquisition begins outside India. Until then, Indian-only filing is sufficient.

## Red flags that should pause the filing

- Knockout search returns a registered mark in your class with confusingly
  similar wording — get an attorney before proceeding.
- The brand "Wander Women" turns out to be a registered trade name of a
  competitor in any travel jurisdiction — pivot the brand before filing.
- A pre-existing common-law (unregistered but used in commerce) mark in
  your industry — first-use rights may pre-date your filing in jurisdictions
  that recognize them. Investigate before launching publicly.
